Our Story

WHO WE ARE

Rideflux was born from a simple idea: electric off-road riding shouldn't be boring. We set out to bring the most capable, most exciting electric bikes to riders who refuse to settle.

We source only from the world's top electric bike manufacturers — partnering closely to ensure every bike we sell is ready to shred. From first-timers to seasoned motocross veterans, we've got a bike that fits the way you ride.

Our team rides what we sell. Every model in our lineup has been tested on real trails, real hills, and real terrain — not just spec sheets. When you buy from Rideflux, you're buying confidence.
